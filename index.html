Hi! I am Munachiso. I am an MSc in Machine learning at MBZUAI, supervised by Prof. Salman Khan, as I work at the intersection of object detection and continual learning.

I got a BSc in CS at NYU, and worked there with Prof. Farah Shamout, on adversarial attacks and defenses for chest radiography.

I am currently contributing to the Sauti Project, a project I originally coined and proposed to our team, which came to life with a modest $8000 USD grant from Deep Learning Indaba. We are trying to teach computers how to talk in local African accents as a means to improve computer based learning for a plurality of African students.

One of my first deep learning projects was a mobile app that interprets sign language spelling into plain alphabet.
